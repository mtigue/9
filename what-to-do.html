When a Claim Occurs


  • Get Help: call 911, EMT, etc.
  • Help the Injured: Remember Med Pay
  • Gather info: witnesses, injured, names, phone numbers
  • Document Property Damage. Gather waivers, weather reports, flight summary.
  • Take photos
  • Report claim to your insurer
  • Is an FAA Report required?
  • DO NOT ADMIT LIABILITY OR PAY ANYTHING
